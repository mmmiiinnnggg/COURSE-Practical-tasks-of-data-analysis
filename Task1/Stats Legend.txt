Основные статистики:

GP - Games Played
MPG - Minutes Per Game
FG% - Field Goals Percentage
3P% - 3-Points Percentage
FT% - Free Throws Percentage
TOV - Turnovers
PF - Personal Fouls
RPG - Rebounds Per Game
APG - Assists Per Game
SPG - Steals Per Game
BPG - Blocks Per Game
PPG - Points Per Game


Статистики, которые не будем учитывать:

FGM - Field Goals Made
FGA - Field Goals Attempt
3PM - 3-Points Made
3PA  - 3-Points Attempt
FTM - Free Throws Made
FTA - Free Throws Attempt
ORB - Offensive Rebound (Per Game)
DRB - Defensive Rebound (Per Game)
